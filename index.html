What is a Make·a·thon?

Hardware Hackathon

Unlike traditional hackathons, UMake provides Arduino kits, sensors, and tools to help you build physical devices that solve real-world problems.

Collaborate

Form a team of 2-4 to bring your craziest prototype ideas to life based on the given theme.

Prizes

Projects will be compiled at the end of the competition. The best team projects will be in it to win prizes!

FAQ

Who can apply?

UMake is open to everyone! We welcome beginners and veterans alike. We will have experienced members available at any point to help give advice.

What if I don't have hardware?

No problem! All the necessary hardware will be provided to you. You may use your own additional tools (3D printers, extra materials) subject to the rules and guidelines.

What is the registration fee?

Registration, hardware, and building materials are free of cost. Those who are interested in pizza lunch on day 1 or day 2 may have to submit a $5 pizza fee.
